I'm a M.S. student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California. Previously, I conducted research on few-shot learning for disease prediction and biomarker discovery at the Bioinformatics and Machine Learning Lab, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Kyuri Jo. Currently, I work on digital pathology with whole-slide images, focusing on efficient and generalizable learning, and on immunology-driven protein design for drug discovery.
